# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Visitors arriving through a direct link or a social preview who want to encounter Vinicius Isumi's photographs away from the compression, noise, and incentives of a social feed.

## Product Purpose

ARCHIVE is a public photographic collection. It gives each body of work a durable, shareable home where the image determines the pace. Success means a visitor can move from index to photograph with complete focus and leave with a tangible sense of the author behind the work.

## Brand Personality

Severe, luminous, exact.

## Anti-references

It must not resemble an Instagram clone, a creator dashboard, a stock-photo catalogue, a scrapbook, a retro film preset, or a soft editorial magazine. It must not use decoration to compete with the photographs, nor mimic a generic dark portfolio.

## Design Principles

- Let darkness frame the photograph rather than become an effect of its own.
- Make orientation immediate: the visitor should always know where they are and how to return.
- Use scale, rules, and restraint to give every collection an authored physical presence.
- Make the photograph opening feel deliberate and cinematic, never like a utility modal.
- Publish without engagement mechanics, artificial scarcity, or interface noise.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG AA contrast and keyboard-operability expectations. Respect reduced-motion preferences, require meaningful alternative text for every photograph, and preserve focus through the lightbox experience.
